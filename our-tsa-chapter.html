The FCCPS TSA Chapter

Great achievements. Great teams.

In the Falls Church City Public Schools, we have chapters at both our High School, George Mason, and at its counterpart, Mary Ellen Henderson Middle School. While these chapters' teams do not mix as dictated by the rules of the Technology Student Association, we collaborate on numerous parts of the TSA experience. We travel to competitions together, meet together, and go to dinner together after meetings and while attending TSA conventions.

As a chapter, we have won several notable awards. Last year (2014-2015), we won first place in the state of Virginia in Community Service Video. At the National confrence, four of our members (Casandre Rice, Erik Boesen, Evan Lawrence, and Colter Adams) recieved their Gold Acievement Awards. Additionally, our Middle School chapter won the Chapter of Excellence award. More about last year's achievements here.

Our High School chapter was established at the beginning of this year, since our older Middle School members had advanced and of course wanted to continue participating in the TSA organization. Member Casandre Rice collaborated with our adult leader, Mr. Ray Wu-Rorrer, to establish a TSA chapter at the high school.
